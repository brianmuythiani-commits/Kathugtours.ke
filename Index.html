About Kathug Guider

Kathug Guider was founded to bring travelers closer to the breathtaking beauty of Kenya’s coast and jungles. From the historic streets of Mombasa, the serene beaches of Malindi, to the cultural heritage of Lamu — our agency connects you with authentic African experiences. We specialize in safaris, private tours, outdoor adventures, and wildlife explorations, all while offering the best accommodations and hospitality.

Main Tourist Attractions

Mombasa

Discover rich Swahili culture, Fort Jesus, and pristine white-sand beaches.

Malindi

Home to coral reefs, marine parks, and vibrant resorts for relaxation and adventure.

Lamu

Step back in time with ancient Swahili architecture, dhow sailing, and cultural charm.

Safari Adventures

Experience Kenya’s wildlife up close with multi-day safari tours and private guides.

Kenya’s Famous Game Reserves

Maasai Mara Reserve

World-renowned for the Great Migration and home to lions, cheetahs, and wildebeests.

Tsavo National Park

One of the largest game parks in the world, famous for red elephants and vast savannahs.

Amboseli National Park

Known for large herds of elephants with the backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro.

Samburu Reserve

A unique semi-arid reserve with rare wildlife like Grevy’s zebra and reticulated giraffes.
